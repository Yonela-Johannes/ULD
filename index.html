What we are about

Lwazi Learning Development is a Non-Profit-organization, our aim is to empower the youngest most vurnerable children from poor homes.

Most important, Life Skills and basic education has the most fundamental power to transform the lives of Children.

Foundational Education is linked to all development goals such as reducing hunger, fighting the spread of deseases,, poverty, encouraging economic growth and creating peace.

About us

We bring children together and expose them to other views of life, than the views that they grew up into
We expose children to healthy positive role-models, and the importance of self-development and self-knowing, awareness and the fundamentals of education.
Ulwazi means 'Knowledge, in Xhosa. And it was founded by Lumka Johannes. Lwazi was founded in June 2018

Lumka Johannes

Lumka Johannes grew up in Townships, in the most poorest South-African communities around Eastern Cape and Cape Town. Where parents oftenly work in poorly paid jobs which bring instability in their homes and wellbeing. And while they are faced many spiritual, emotional and financial problems they have to raise and teach their chilren. Poverty stricken and faced with obstacles around home and in the community trying their best to keep up to day to day challenges.
