ABOUT US

Ulwazi means 'Knowledge', in Xhosa. It was founded by Lumka Johannes.
We bring children together and expose them to other views of life, than the views that they grew up in. We expose children to healthy positive role-models, and the importance of self-development and self-knowing,

Learning without limits.

Our main focus and core purpose is to keep young children off the streets where they are prey to gangsterism, away from drugs and alcohol usage. To mold a healthy and a looking forward positive thinking youth. Children who will create a more happy and better living for themselves and their families.

ABOUT ME

LUMKA JOHANNES

Lumka Johannes grew up in Townships, in the most poorest South-African communities around Eastern Cape and Cape Town. Where parents oftenly work in poorly paid jobs which bring instability in their homes and wellbeing. And while they are faced many spiritual, emotional and financial problems they have to raise and teach their chilren. Poverty stricken and faced with obstacles around home and in the community trying their best to keep up to day to day challenges.
